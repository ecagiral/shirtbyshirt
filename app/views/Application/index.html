SBS Textile known as ShirtbyShirt specializes in the production of fine men and women shirts. The company was established in 2000 aiming to create innovative and high quality products. In the previous 12 years the company has become the market leader with an increasing capacity of production and meeting demand of 10 million garments per year which includes 9 million shirts and 1 million trousers in European, American and Turkish markets. With its talented design team of highly experienced people, the brand establishes the collection by following up global trends and developments, never failing to attend all the trade shows all over the world. Quality, creativity and professionalism is enhanced in every way along with reliable service and competitive price policy. As a design office, ShirtbyShirt is able to satisfy all of its customers’ needs and provide creative solutions to any kind of demands with its extensive experience regarding fabric research and development with the principle of highest standard production.

FACILITIES

ShirtbyShirt’s rapidly growing company’s head office is currently operating by marketing, finance, sourcing, merchandising, branding and design departments, quality control, pattern making and proto sewing lab, generate a large team of 200 people.

ShirtbyShirt showrooms consist of wide product range in men’s, women’s and childrenwear. The men’s shirt showroom possess smart, casual, fashionable, garment dyed, garment washed and many diffirent styles. Every month the design team work on 300 pieces strong collection for SBS showroom. Based on the demand created by the customers SBS introduced pants as a new to its customers in 2010 and expanded its product portfolio with non denim, chino, garment dyed pants and shorts.

However women’s showroom created with the latest trends and different styles with customers’ need by the design team with 100 pieces for women‘s wear in a month.

SBS is also ambitious in children’s clothing which is continuing to grow rapidly is in progress. Each season 100 pieces for children’s wear appear in showroom.
